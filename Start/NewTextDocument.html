About Us

UTI-United Travellers Of India

Started on 31st dec 2018, with an aim to unite all type of travellers under one roof, we started United Travellers of India. It’s been 3 years now we have organised more than 500 successful trek/ trip events. With more than 20 k+ happy customers. Now after 3 years we’re into local Sahyadri treks, Trips in Maharashtra, We do domestic tours in northern & southern India & aiming for international travel in next year. We had 4 main factors which included Happiness of our Customers, No Addiction on Treks & Trips, Experienced Team & Last but not the least safety of our customers. It will be our pleasure if you join us on our events. We will guarantee you the best service in the market!

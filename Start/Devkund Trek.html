DEVKUND WATERFALL TREK

Trek Code-101



Overview

Devkund Waterfall is one of the most beautiful waterfall located near Bhira, in Raigad district, Maharastra, India. It is a 'plunge' waterfall pouring massive amounts of water on the rocky surface underneath. It is the most scenic combination of 3 waterfalls & is also known as the origin of Kundalika river. The height of the Devkund Waterfall Trek is 2700 ft. approx.

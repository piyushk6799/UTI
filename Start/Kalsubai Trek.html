MAHARASHTRA'S HIGHEST PEAK... KALSUBAI

Trek Code-100



Overview

Kalsubai peak also known as the Everest of Maharashtra in the western ghats of Maharashtra. I t is the highest peak in Sahyadri mountain range of Maharashtra at an altitude of 1646 mts. Kalsubai Shikhar is situated on the border of Ahmadnagar and Nashik districts. There is a small temple of local deity situated on the summit. During the trek one witness the views of peaks such as Alang, Madangad, Kulang, Ratangad,& Harishchandragad from the highest point.
